Why this Data

This data has a little bit of everything, it piques my interest by just looking around and lets me find interesting data about what happens around the city, questions like "why did I buy an expensive lock for my bike?", "why do so many of my friend's cars get broken into?", "why does my university seem to send fairly frequent reports about staying safe?", it also pulls up several questions About mental health in the city, in terms of suicide, it shows when it happens the most and where, it may even show a tendency. Unfortunately it does not really halp answer the why of this, but it might identify areas or people that are in need of care.
An exploration through this dataset can produce many questions, and might even guide an interested investifator towards some answers.
