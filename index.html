Police Data Narrative

2020 has not been a good year so far. As I write this we are staying in our homes because of the Covid-19 pandemic affecting the world. From where I live in San Francisco, in the Inner Richmond, I hear sirens go by several times a day (and night). It made me start to think if there were more sirens going off than usual, or is it that since I am now at home all day I just hear the normal amount of sirens going about.
I now want to see what has the Police Department been dealing with these past weeks, and what has the difference been in their responses compared to the same time last year. Now that everyone is home all the time, I believe that there are fewer amount of break-ins to homes, but maybe more towards commercial locations, or maybe everyone staying indoors has reduced overall calls to the police department.
Given the unique situation that we are in, I would like to see if the type and amount of calls to the police department has varied in comparison with the same time last year by exploring the subcategories by date ranges.
